# ServiQ - Smart Local Services, On Demand

**_ServiQ_** is a hyperlocal mobile service marketplace that connects people with trusted nearby professionals in real time, enabling fast, reliable booking of essential services like home repairs, tutoring, event support, digital support, and health & wellness support through a seamless, location-based app experience.

### _"It’s an app to instantly find and book trusted local service providers near you—like electricians, tutors, or photographers—all in one place."_</br></br>
